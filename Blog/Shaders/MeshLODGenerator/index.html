Overview

The Mesh LOD Generator is a powerful Unity Editor tool designed to automatically generate Level of Detail (LOD) versions of your 3D models. By reducing triangle counts in a single click, it helps significantly optimize game performance, especially for scenes with high-density geometry. This tool simplifies the optimization pipeline by handling both the mesh decimation and the setup of LODGroup components.

Key Features

  • Intelligent Decimation: High-quality triangle reduction that respects the original silhouette and UV mapping.
  • Batch Processing: Select multiple meshes and generate LODs for all of them simultaneously.
  • Auto LODGroup: Automatically creates a prefab with a configured LODGroup component, sparing you from manual setup.
  • Customizable Levels: Define up to 6 LOD levels with individual reduction ratios and screen-height thresholds.
  • Seamless Integration: Preserves materials, submeshes, and essential mesh data during the process.

Decimation Tech

At the heart of the generator is a sophisticated decimation algorithm that prioritizes edge collapses based on geometric error. It includes specialized toggles to:

  • Preserve UV Seams: Ensures that texture mappings remain intact even at very low triangle counts.
  • Surface Curvature: Maintains the smooth feel of rounded surfaces by protecting high-curvature areas from aggressive reduction.
  • Weld Tolerance: Automatically merges nearby vertices to clean up messy geometry before processing.

Automation Workflow

The tool is designed for high-speed production environments:

  1. Load: Select your meshes and "Add Selected" or drag them directly into the tool window.
  2. Configure: Adjust the LOD levels. For instance, LOD1 at 75% triangles, LOD2 at 50%, and LOD3 at 25%.
  3. Generate: Hit the generate button. The tool creates new mesh assets and a root prefab for each item.
  4. Result: Your optimized assets are ready to be placed in the scene, complete with cross-fading and shadow-casting configurations.
